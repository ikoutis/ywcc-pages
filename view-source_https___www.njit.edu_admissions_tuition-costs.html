Tuition & Costs

For each academic year, the standard cost of attendance as defined by the Student Financial Aid Services (SFAS) Office depends on your program, year in school, residency and housing selection.

Undergraduate Cost of Attendance* for 2024- 2025(two semesters at full-time**)

New Jersey Residents Non-New Jersey Residents
Tuition $16,334 $34,024
Fees $3,640 $3,640
Total Tuition and Fees $19,974 $37,664

** Full-time = 12-19 credits

  • For New Jersey resident and Non-New Jersey resident students living on campus, the value for Housing & Food is $16,450and Additional Indirect Cost is $5,200.
  • For New Jersey resident and Non-New Jersey resident students living with parents, the value for Housing & Food is $9,800 and Additional Indirect Cost is $6,900.
  • For New Jersey resident and Non-New Jersey resident students living off campus, the value for Housing & Food is $17,936and Additional Indirect Cost is $6,900.
  • The value for Books & Supplies is $3,400 (first-year and transfer non-architecture students) and$4,800 (first-year and transfer architecture students).

* Cost of Attendance components include: Tuition & Fees; Housing & Food; Books, Course Materials, Supplies & Equipment; Transportation; Personal Expenses; Federal Loan Fees; and Professional Licensure, Certification, or a First Professional Credential Cost.

Graduate Cost of Attendance* for 2024- 2025(two semesters at full-time**)

New Jersey Residents Non-New Jersey Residents
Tuition $23,076 $33,138
Fees $4,734 $4,734
Total Per Year $27,810 $37,872

** Full-time = 9 credits

  • For New Jersey resident and Non-New Jersey resident students living on campus, the value for Housing & Food is $16,450and Additional Indirect Cost is $6,400.
  • For New Jersey resident and Non-New Jersey resident students living off campus, the value for Housing & Food is $17,936 and Additional Indirect Cost is $9,400.
  • The value for Books & Supplies is $1,600 fornon-architecture studentsand$2,000 for architecture students.

* Cost of Attendance components include: Tuition & Fees; Housing & Food; Books, Course Materials, Supplies & Equipment; Transportation; Personal Expenses; Federal Loan Fees; and Professional Licensure, Certification, or a First Professional Credential Cost.
